# esp32Generic
A generic firmware for esp32 with easy setup, OTA updates, mqtt support and more.

PIN assignments.
* LED: 2
* BUTTON: 13
* ACTORS
** PUMP: 4
** VALVE_OPEN: 26
** VALVE_CLOSE: 27
* SENSORS
** FLOW_TEMP: 32
** RETURN_TEMP: 35
** OUTSIDE_TEMP: 34
* TFT
 ** TFT_MISO 25
 ** TFT_MOSI 23
 ** TFT_SCLK 19  VSPIQ-MISO
 ** TFT_CS 22    VSPIWP
 ** TFT_DC 21    VSPIH2
 ** TFT_RST 18 - VSPICLK
 ** TFT_BL 5 -   VSPICS0
 * PWM
 ** PIN1 27
